\enlargethispage{2ex}
\vspace*{-2pt}

\enlargethispage{2ex}

Given data instances $X$ (with labels $Y$), a \textbf{generative model} is a model of the conditional probability $p(X | Y)$, or, if labels are not available, of $p(X)$.
